# StoryForge Kids TikTok Ad Scripts (Ages 0-12)

These scripts are designed for short-form video (15-30 seconds), optimized for TikTok's fast-paced, visually driven style, and should be set to trending, upbeat background music.

---

## Video 1: The All-in-One Solution

**Concept:** Show a parent struggling to find engaging and educational activities for their children of different ages. Introduce StoryForge Kids as the all-in-one solution.

| Scene | Visuals | On-Screen Text | Audio/Voiceover |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 (0-5s)** | **Fast-paced montage:** Parent looks stressed, juggling a toddler pulling at their sleeve and an older child (approx. 8-10) slumped on the couch, complaining. Quick cuts of different, failed activities (e.g., a broken toy, a half-finished worksheet). | **"One app for all your kids?"** (Appears large and bold) | **Sound Effect:** Frustrated sigh. **VO (Fast, relatable tone):** "Stop juggling three different apps for three different kids." |
| **2 (5-10s)** | Parent swipes through their phone, looking defeated, then stops on the StoryForge Kids app icon. A calm, warm light appears on their face. Quick shot of the app's clean, inviting interface. | **"Ages 0-12. Covered."** | **Music:** Shifts to a hopeful, positive beat. **VO:** "Meet StoryForge Kids. The one app that grows with them." |
| **3 (10-20s)** | **Split Screen:** **Left:** Parent and toddler creating a simple picture book (tapping on pre-set words, adding a photo). **Right:** Older child independently typing a chapter into a more complex template. Quick flash of a "Special Needs/Accessibility" icon. | **"Simple Picture Books"** (on left) **"Complex Chapter Books"** (on right) | **VO:** "From simple tap-to-create stories for your little ones, to full-blown chapter books for your big kids. Even features for special needs." |
| **4 (20-25s)** | The whole family is gathered on the couch, laughing and admiring the different storybooks on a tablet. Close-up on the diverse range of stories created. | **"One App. Endless Stories."** | **Music:** Peaks in a warm, happy melody. **VO:** "End the boredom battle. Start the story." |
| **5 (25-30s)** | Clear shot of the app logo and a finger tapping the "Download" button on an app store screen. | **"StoryForge Kids: Download Now!"** (Clear CTA) | **VO:** "StoryForge Kids: One app, endless stories. Download now!" |

## Video 2: The Creative Journey

**Concept:** Showcase the creative journey from a young child's simple idea to an older child's detailed story, highlighting the app's ability to capture all stages of development.

| Scene | Visuals | On-Screen Text | Audio/Voiceover |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 (0-5s)** | Close-up on a toddler's hand scribbling vigorously with a crayon on a piece of paper. The scribble is abstract and colorful. | **"It all starts here..."** | **Sound Effect:** Crayon on paper. **VO (Gentle, encouraging tone):** "Every epic tale begins with a spark." |
| **2 (5-10s)** | Parent takes a picture of the scribble with their phone. The app instantly converts the scribble into a digital illustration within a StoryForge Kids book template. The toddler points excitedly. | **"Turn Scribbles into Art!"** | **Sound Effect:** Magic chime/whoosh. **VO:** "With StoryForge Kids, you can turn their first scribbles into their first published book." |
| **3 (10-20s)** | Transition to an older child (approx. 10-12) sitting at a desk, thoughtfully sketching a storyboard in a notebook, then smoothly transitioning to typing a detailed, multi-chapter narrative on the app. Show a feature for adding custom, detailed illustrations. | **"Storyboarding. Chapters. World-Building."** | **Music:** Shifts to a more focused, slightly dramatic score. **VO:** "Then, watch them grow into master storytellers, building complex worlds and detailed narratives." |
| **4 (20-25s)** | **Split Screen:** **Left:** The toddler's simple, colorful, 3-page picture book. **Right:** The older child's sophisticated chapter book with a cover and table of contents. Both have the StoryForge Kids logo. | **"From First Words to Epic Tales."** | **VO:** "Two kids. One app. Infinite potential." |
| **5 (25-30s)** | Clear shot of the app logo and the final CTA. | **"Create with StoryForge Kids. Link in Bio."** | **VO:** "From first words to epic tales. Create with StoryForge Kids." |

| Scene | Visuals | On-Screen Text | Audio/Voiceover |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

Mixed-Age Classroom?"** | **VO (Upbeat, professional tone):** "Teaching multiple ages means multiple lesson plans. It's a lot." |
| **2 (5-10s)** | The teacher/parent opens StoryForge Kids. Show a feature where they can set a single writing prompt (e.g., "Write about a trip to the moon"), but the app automatically adjusts the required complexity, vocabulary, and sentence structure based on the child's profile. | **"Differentiated Learning Made Easy."** | **Sound Effect:** Satisfied click. **VO:** "StoryForge Kids simplifies differentiation. Vocabulary Integration. Progress Tracking."** | **VO:** "Track progress, integrate curriculum-specific vocabulary, and empower every student, from emergent readers to advanced writers." |
| **4 (20-25s)** | The teacher/parent is smiling, holding up a tablet showing a beautiful, diverse portfolio of student-created books. They give a thumbs-up to the camera. | **"Simplify Your Lesson Planning."** | **Music:** Inspiring, educational-sounding music. **VO:** "Turn required writing practice into a passion project." |
| **5 (25-30s)** | Clear shot of the app logo and the final CTA. | **"StoryForge Kids: The Essential Educator's Tool. Try it Free!"** | **VO:** "StoryForge Kids: The essential tool for every educator. Try it free today!" |
