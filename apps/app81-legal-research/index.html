📄 Acme Corp Service Agreement — Section 4: Liability

4.1 Limitation of Liability. In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, exemplary, or consequential damages, however caused and under any theory of liability, whether in contract, strict liability, or tort.

4.2 Aggregate Liability Cap. The total aggregate liability of Provider shall not exceed the amounts paid in the three (3) months preceding the claim, regardless of the form of action or the basis of the claim.

4.3 Indemnification. Each party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other party from and against any claims arising from its own negligence or willful misconduct.

4.4 Force Majeure. Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from acts beyond the reasonable control of such party, including acts of God, labor disputes, or government actions of any kind whatsoever.
