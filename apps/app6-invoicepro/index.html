⚡ Features

Everything you need to run your freelance business

From your first invoice to your tax return — InvoicePro handles the financial side of freelancing.

📄

Beautiful Invoices in 60 Seconds

Create and send polished, branded invoices in under a minute. Add your logo, choose from 20+ templates, and set payment terms. Clients can pay by card, bank transfer, or PayPal with one click.

⏰

Automated Payment Reminders

Set it and forget it. InvoicePro automatically sends polite payment reminders at 7, 3, and 1 day before due date — and escalating reminders for overdue invoices. Cut late payments by 73%.

💳

Online Payments Built-In

Accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers (ACH), and PayPal directly from your invoice. Funds deposited to your bank in 1-2 business days. Stripe-powered, bank-grade security.

📊

Expense Tracking

Snap photos of receipts, categorize expenses, and track your business costs automatically. Connect your bank account for automatic transaction import. Never miss a tax deduction again.

🧮

Tax Preparation

Export profit & loss statements, expense reports, and tax summaries with one click. Integrates with QuickBooks, FreshBooks, and TurboTax. Makes tax season genuinely painless.

📈

Revenue Analytics

Track your income trends, top clients, most profitable project types, and payment velocity. See your business health at a glance and make data-driven decisions about your rates and clients.

🔄

Recurring Invoices

Set up monthly retainer invoices that send automatically. Perfect for ongoing client relationships. Automatic late fees can be applied after a set number of days, compounding at the rate you choose.

🌍

Multi-Currency & Global

Invoice clients in 50+ currencies. Automatic exchange rate conversion. Support for international payment methods including SEPA, iDEAL, Bancontact, and more for European clients.

🔗

Integrations

Connect with Stripe, PayPal, QuickBooks, Xero, Zapier, Slack, Google Drive, Dropbox, and 50+ other tools. Your invoicing synced with your entire business workflow.
