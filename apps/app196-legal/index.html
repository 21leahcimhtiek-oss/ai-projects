Employment Agreement

EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between [Company Name] ("Employer") and [Employee Name] ("Employee") effective as of January 1, 2024.

1. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with 30 days notice. Employee acknowledges that upon termination, all intellectual property created during employment shall remain the exclusive property of Employer.

2. NON-COMPETE
Employee agrees not to work for direct competitors within a 50-mile radius for 12 months following termination.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee shall maintain the confidentiality of all trade secrets, customer lists, and proprietary information.

4. BENEFITS
Employee shall be entitled to standard benefits including health insurance, paid time off, and retirement plan participation in accordance with company policy.

🔍 Key Findings

⚠️
Non-Compete Clause

12-month restriction may be unenforceable in certain jurisdictions. Consider reducing to 6 months.

⚡
IP Assignment

Broad IP assignment clause is standard but may need clarification for pre-existing works.

✓
Benefits Section

Benefits provision is fair and compliant with standard employment practices.
